Pricing

Simple, honest pricing. No surprises.

We believe every Swiss business owner should know exactly what they are paying for before they commit to anything. So here is everything, laid out clearly.

How it works

Pay once. Then a little each month.

Building a website is a one-time project. Keeping it healthy, up to date and working perfectly is an ongoing job. We have split these into two separate things so you only pay for what you actually need.

1

You pay once for the website

We design, build and launch your site. Once it is live, you own it completely. The creation fee covers everything from the first conversation to the moment your site goes public.

2

A monthly care plan

For a small fixed amount each month, we take care of everything your website needs to keep running properly.

3

No hidden fees, ever

What you see on this page is what you pay. There are no setup charges, no platform fees and no surprises on your invoice.
